Wittle Defender Hero Tier List (June 2025)

The definitive ranking of all defenders. Find the best units for your team.

Tier Defenders

Overpowered (S Tier)

Sword Saint

  • + Deals high damage even without full upgrades.
  • + Excellent targeting system for focusing on bosses.

God Ruler

  • + Highest potential damage in the game with a few upgrades.
  • + Massive AoE damage for clearing waves.

Robot

  • + Second-highest damage dealer, great against high HP targets.
  • + Can be upgraded to become a powerful frontline tank.

Extremely Strong (A Tier)

Demon Spawn

  • + Tons of single-target damage, perfect for bosses.
  • - Relies on RNG for good upgrades, slow base attack speed.

Fire Witch

  • + High damage against waves of enemies.
  • - Skill has a long cooldown.

Swordmaster

  • + Optimal against waves due to high damage.
  • - Not good against bosses.

Blazing Archer

  • + Provides utility by spawning units.
  • + Good damage against both waves and bosses.

Ice Demon

  • + Summons units to distract and tank for the team.
  • - Relies on the team for consistent DPS.

Ice Witch

  • + Can freeze enemies, providing crowd control.
  • - Relies heavily on upgrades to be effective.

Somewhat Useful (B Tier)

Cat Assassin

  • + Good single-target damage.
  • - Upgrades are not worth investing in.

Night Baron

  • + Valuable for his powerful chain link synergy.
  • - Not a strong character on his own.

Thunder Pharaoh

  • + Pairs well with Robot for enhanced damage.
  • - Low initial damage.
