How to Redeem Codes

Follow these simple steps to claim your rewards.

  1. 1
    Launch Wittle Defender and tap your Profile Avatar in the top-left corner.
  2. 2
    On your profile page, tap the Copy button next to your User ID.
  3. 3
    Visit the official Wittle Defender Redeem Center:Go to Redeem Center
  4. 4
    Paste your User ID, enter a code from our list, and type in the on-page verification code.
  5. 5
    Click Redeem, then return to the game and check your in-game mailbox to claim your rewards!
