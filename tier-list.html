Wittle Defender Hero Tier List

The definitive ranking of all defenders for the June 2025 meta. This list is based on community data and expert analysis to help you build the most powerful team.

TierDefenders
SSword Saint, Robot, God Ruler
ADemon Spawn, Fire Witch, Swordmaster, Blazing Archer, Ice Demon, Ice Witch
BCat Assassin, Night Baron, Thunder Pharaoh, Fire Mage, High Priest
CDemon Hunter, Ice Mage
DFrankenstein, Ice Wolf Pup, and other Epic/Common units.

Overpowered (S Tier)

Units in this tier are the best in the game due to having good AoE spells and high damage. They can clear large waves without any issues.

Sword Saint

  • + Deals high damage even without full upgrades.
  • + Excellent targeting system for focusing on bosses.

God Ruler

  • + Highest potential damage in the game.
  • + Massive AoE damage for clearing waves.

Robot

  • + Second-highest damage dealer.
  • + Can be upgraded to become a powerful tank.

Extremely Strong (A Tier)

Demon Spawn

  • + Tons of single-target damage for bosses.
  • - Relies on RNG for good upgrades.

Fire Witch

  • + High damage against waves.
  • - Skill has a long cooldown.

Swordmaster

  • + Optimal against waves.
  • - Not good against bosses.

Blazing Archer

  • + Provides utility by spawning units.

Ice Demon

  • + Summons units to distract and tank.
  • - Relies on the team for DPS.

Ice Witch

  • + Can freeze enemies for crowd control.
  • - Relies heavily on upgrades.

Somewhat Useful (B Tier)

Cat Assassin

  • + Good single-target damage.
  • - Upgrades are not worth investing in.

Night Baron

  • + Valuable for his chain link synergy.
  • - Not a strong character on his own.

Thunder Pharaoh

  • + Pairs well with Robot.
  • - Low initial damage.

Only Works in Certain Cases (C Tier)

These units do not perform well if not placed in a strong team. It's best to avoid these units as soon as you get a better option.

Demon Hunter

  • + Scattershot upgrade is useful for AoE.
  • - Low damage even when fully upgraded.

Ice Mage

  • + Has a chain link with Ice Witch for high AoE damage.
  • - Not a good character on her own.

Do Not Deploy (D Tier)

As the name suggests, do not deploy these defenders. This is due to their low stats, weak upgrades, and low damage.

Frankenstein

  • - Does not deal good damage.
  • - His skill is useless.

Ice Wolf Pup

  • - Quickly gets useless due to low damage.
  • - Upgrades aren't worth getting.
